Welcome to CosmicShutter!

Astrophotography gives me chills every time I point my lens at the night sky. The stars, planets, and deep sky objects seem so distant, yet so close through the camera. Each shot captures a moment of the universe’s beauty, leaving me in awe. Sharing these glimpses of the cosmos allows others to feel the wonder I experience under the stars.

- Erika Denise Encinas
