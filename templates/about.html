About Me

I have been capturing the night sky since 2020 under the clear skies of Larap, Camarines Norte, Philippines. I am a cum laude graduate in Astronomy, majoring in Astrophysics, from Rizal Technological University. Driven by a deep curiosity about the universe, I continually explore astronomy while sharing the beauty of the cosmos through my photography.

For inquiries or collaborations, please feel free to contact me at erikadenise79@gmail.com.
